The Pipe Band Competion
Dollar Academy have achieved the best of the best award in pipes

Each year each school gets a chance to host the pipe band competition. in competition, bands compete against bands of similar ability levels and carefully perform required tunes.What the Judges look for in competion. Is the band should sound as one, with well-tuned instruments and steady tempos. At the end of a performance a sharp cutoff, where all pipers cease at once

Inter School Sports day
